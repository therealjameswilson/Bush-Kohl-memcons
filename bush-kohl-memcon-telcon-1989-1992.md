# Bush–Kohl Memcons/Telcons (1989–1992)

Working list for FRUS annotation support, using records sourced from **NARA / George H.W. Bush Presidential Library holdings** (Brent Scowcroft Collection, including Presidential Telcon Files / Presidential Memcons references).

## Chronological list

1. **May 30, 1990** — **Memorandum of Telephone Conversation**: “Telephone Call from Chancellor Helmut Kohl of the Federal Republic of Germany” (Kohl → Bush).  
   - Repository/source basis: George H.W. Bush Presidential Library holdings (NARA).

2. **June 1, 1990** — **Memorandum of Telephone Conversation**: “Telephone Call [by President Bush] to Chancellor Helmut Kohl of the Federal Republic of Germany” (Bush → Kohl).  
   - Repository/source basis: George H.W. Bush Presidential Library, Memcons and Telcons File (NARA).

3. **February 18, 1991** — **White House Memorandum of Telephone Conversation**: “Telcon with Chancellor Kohl of Germany.”  
   - Repository/source basis: Bush Presidential Library, Brent Scowcroft Collection, Presidential Telcon Files, FOIA 2009-0275-S (NARA).

4. **February 23, 1991** — **White House Memorandum of Telephone Conversation**: “Telcon with Chancellor Kohl of Germany.”  
   - Repository/source basis: Bush Presidential Library, Brent Scowcroft Collection, Presidential Telcon Files, FOIA 2009-0275-S (NARA).

5. **March 21, 1992** — **White House Memorandum of Conversation**: “Meeting with Chancellor Helmut Kohl of Germany.”  
   - Repository/source basis: Bush Presidential Records, Office of Brent Scowcroft files, Presidential Memcons subseries (NARA).
