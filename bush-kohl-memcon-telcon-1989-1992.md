## Chronological list

1. **May 30, 1990** — **Memorandum of Telephone Conversation**: “Telephone Call from Chancellor Helmut Kohl of the Federal Republic of Germany” (Kohl → Bush).

   - Citation note on source page: George H.W. Bush Presidential Library.

2. **June 1, 1990** — **Memorandum of Telephone Conversation**: “Telephone Call [by President Bush] to Chancellor Helmut Kohl of the Federal Republic of Germany” (Bush → Kohl).  
   - Source page: https://nsarchive.gwu.edu/document/20380-national-security-archive-doc-18-memorandum
   - Citation note on source page: George H.W. Bush Presidential Library, Memcons and Telcons File (MDR release).

3. **February 18, 1991** — **White House Memorandum of Telephone Conversation**: “Telcon with Chancellor Kohl of Germany.”  
   - Source page: https://nsarchive.gwu.edu/index.php/document/24690-document-04-white-house-memorandum-telephone-conversation-telcon-chancellor-kohl
   - Citation note on source page: Bush Presidential Library, Brent Scowcroft Collection, Presidential Telcon Files, FOIA 2009-0275-S.

4. **February 23, 1991** — **White House Memorandum of Telephone Conversation**: “Telcon with Chancellor Kohl of Germany.”  
   - Source page: https://nsarchive.gwu.edu/document/21068-1991-02-23-bush-kohl-memcon
   - Citation note on source page: Bush Presidential Library, Brent Scowcroft Collection, Presidential Telcon Files, FOIA 2009-0275-S.

5. **March 21, 1992** — **White House Memorandum of Conversation**: “Meeting with Chancellor Helmut Kohl of Germany.”  
   - Source page: https://nsarchive.gwu.edu/document/30416-document-8-white-house-memorandum-conversation-subject-meeting-chancellor-helmut
   - Citation note on source page: Bush Presidential Records, Office of Brent Scowcroft files, Presidential Memcons subseries.
